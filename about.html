COMPANY PROFILE

SIDRA Equipments Trading Est. was established in 2002 in Dammam, the industrial city of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We have a diversified product range specializing in Contracting and trading of industrial materials, hardware, plumbing and electrical suppliers.
Over the years, SIDRA has accomplished a booming expansion based on a substantial business portfolio.
We have a good reputation and excellent track records in supplying quality materials and services for industrial and commercial projects. SIDRA has all the potential to assure the prime products and services to meet the challenging needs of the customers all over the province.
We offer the best service for our customers through a talented and experienced team of sales.
